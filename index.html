Industry Exhibition Program

The IEEE Industrial Electronics Society (IES) is establishing an Industry Exhibition in the flagship research conference IEEE IECON 2023 in Singapore at Marina Bay Sands.

We bring together Technical Experts and Industry to advance Industrial technologies and products. IES technologies span all those for industrial solutions, including sensors, robotics, control systems, automation, and power, along with expertise in the needed infrastructure, including networking, informatics, processing, and standards.

Audience: Industry engineers, leaders in Information and Communication Technologies (ICT), Operations, and Automation/Manufacturing.

Why exhibit at Industry Exhibition:

  • Major opportunity for new customers engaging with a broad network of customers and suppliers
  • Training opportunities for engineers and corporate leadership in the latest technologies and their use in Industrial applications.
  • Recruitment: Access high-caliber graduates participating in IECON.

In addition to IEEE IECON’s 2,500 technical experts, researchers, and graduate attendees, Industry Days will look to attract practitioners and executives across the industry engaged in industrial electronics products. Including Operations, Information, and Manufacturing.

Exhibition Opportunities

Industry Days is a unique opportunity to showcase your company’s products and services. The exhibition space is located at the Marina Bay Sands Expo & Convention Center, Singapore. Exhibitors will have the opportunity to meet with industry leaders and decision-makers from around the world. The exhibition will be open to all conference attendees and in the same venue as the conference sessions.

EXHIBIT your solutions. Customizable booth packages begin at 1,000 USD with multiple spaces, passes, and sponsorship options available.

NETWORK with customers. Focused networking and sponsorship opportunities to be available to engage with your customers.

LEAD the conversation. Multiple opportunities to participate in education and other thought leadership during the event.
